About

Readify is a tool designed to support people with dyslexia by making reading smoother, clearer, and less stressful. It transforms difficult text into a simpler, more readable form while keeping the original meaning intact. Readify also includes a built-in text-to-speech feature that lets users listen to any content they struggle to read, helping them learn and understand at their own pace. With a clean interface, accessible design, and helpful reading tools, Readify aims to give every user a comfortable and confident reading experience.
