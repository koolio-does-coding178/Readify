What Is Dyslexia

Dyslexia is a learning difference that affects reading, writing, and spelling, but it is not related to intelligence. Worldwide, over 500 million people suffer from dyslexia, making it one of the most common learning challenges. Raising awareness is important to reduce stigma, provide support, and create inclusive environments in schools, workplaces, and communities. With understanding, the right strategies, and tools, people with dyslexia can thrive and reach their full potential.
